Pocket Ledger is built on a few non-negotiable beliefs about personal finance software.

## Local-first

Your financial data stays on your device. Pocket Ledger uses a local SQLite database stored in your AppData directory. There is no cloud sync, no remote server, and no account creation required.

If you want a backup, copy the database file. If you want to move devices, transfer the file. You own your data completely.

## Privacy by design

Zero telemetry. Zero analytics. Zero external connections unless you explicitly opt in. Pocket Ledger never phones home, never tracks usage patterns, and never shares data with third parties.

This is not a feature toggle — it is a design constraint baked into the architecture.

## Envelope budgeting

Every dollar gets a job. When income arrives, you allocate it across named envelopes — Rent, Groceries, Savings, Fun Money. You spend only from envelopes, and when an envelope is empty, spending in that category stops.

This methodology is proven, simple, and makes overspending structurally difficult.

## Goal-oriented

Pocket Ledger is designed around what you are saving for, not just what you are spending on. Savings goals with visual progress tracking keep you focused on outcomes rather than just transactions.
